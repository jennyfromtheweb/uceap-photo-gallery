Adventures in Scotland
and Beyond

Hi, I'm Jenny! During my junior year of university in 2017, I spent a semester studying abroad at the University of St Andrews as part of UCEAP. Here are a few photos I took during my four months in Europe.

    • Scroll on! (Click on any photo to enlarge.)

St Andrews

The birthplace of golf and home to the oldest university in Scotland. These photos showcase the beauty of the small seaside town I called home, from its brilliant golden flowers to its majestic church towers.

Edinburgh, Glasgow, & the Highlands

Edinburgh (where Arthur's seat offers a satisfying overlook of the city), Glasgow (where the Gothic architecture is spectacularly intricate), and the Highlands (where I got to pet a hairy coo!).

Manchester & the Peak District

For the 2017 UCEAPUKI Spring Trip, we headed off to Manchester, taking in the sights of Chatsworth House and the surrounding natural beauty of the Peak District.

London

Strolling through the bustling streets of London never gets boring. Highlights included Big Ben, Somerset House, Buckingham Palace, and the view of the city from the Sky Garden.

Paris

City of Light, City of Love. The magic of Paris is captured in its romantic love locks, awe-inspiring architecture, and evening lights. From the Eiffel Tower to the Louvre, the city is a testament to art and beauty.

Brussels & Bruges

With gilded ornamentation in Brussels and quaint little neighborhoods in Bruges, Belgium offers much to explore. And no visit to Belgium is complete without a taste of their waffles, fries, chocolates, and beers.

Amsterdam

There's something inspiring about how the bright lights of Amsterdam reflect off the canal waters. It's no wonder why so many artists set up residence here.

Thanks for visiting!
