Adventures Abroad:
Scotland and Beyond

Hi, I'm Jenny! In the spring semester of my junior year, I said farewell to California and departed my home university of UC Berkeley to study at the University of St Andrews in Scotland as part of the UCEAP exchange program. Here are a few photos I took during my four months in Europe, capturing some of my favorite moments traveling abroad.

St Andrews

The birthplace of golf and the oldest university in Scotland. These photos showcase the beauty of the small seaside town I called home, from its brilliant natural landscape to its majestic church towers.
January - May 2017.

Anstruther & the Isle of May

Just outside of St Andrews is a small town by the name of Anstruther, known for their mouth-watering fish and chips. An hour away by boat lies the Isle of May, a spectacularly lush island inhabitated by puffins.
April 15, 2017.

Edinburgh, Glasgow, & the Highlands

The diverse features of Scotland include Edinburgh (where Arthur's seat offers a satisfying overlook of the city), Glasgow (where the Gothic architecture is wonderfully intricate), and the Highlands (where I pet a hairy coo!).
April 22-24, 2017.

Manchester & the Peak District

For the 2017 UCEAPUKI Spring Trip, we headed off to Manchester, taking in the sights of Chatsworth House and the surrounding natural beauty of the Peak District.
April 7-9, 2017.

London

Strolling through the bustling streets of London never gets boring. Highlights include Big Ben, Somerset House, Buckingham Palace, and the view of the city from the Sky Garden.
March 18-21, 2017.

Paris

City of Light, City of Love. The magic of Paris is captured in its romantic love locks, awe-inspiring architecture, and evening lights. From the Eiffel Tower to the Louvre, the city is a testament to art and beauty.
March 21-23, 2017.

Brussels & Bruges

With gilded ornamentation in Brussels and quaint little neighborhoods in Bruges, Belgium offers much to explore. And no visit to Belgium is complete without a taste of their waffles, fries, chocolates, and beers.
March 23-25, 2017.

Amsterdam

There's something inspiring about how the bright lights of Amsterdam reflect off the canal waters. It's no wonder why so many artists set up residence here.
March 25-27, 2017.

Thanks for visiting!
